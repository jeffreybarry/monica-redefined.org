Monica Redefined

The Tripp Tapes

Linda Tripp befriended and betrayed Monica by recording nearly 20 hours of personal conversations over three months in 1997 and 1998. Tripp became a key witness for Independent Counsel's Kenneth Starr. Tripp, who was 27 years older than Monica, worked in the Pentagon’s public affairs office. She acted like a mentor to Monica before she turned over the tapes to Starr.
