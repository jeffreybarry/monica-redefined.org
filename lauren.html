Trying to Live a Normal Life

What did she do after the scandal?

Struggling to regain her footing after the publicity of the trial and affair, Monica experimented with several different business ventures in the late 90s-2000s. These include a guest host spot on Saturday Night Live, her own handbag line, and a role as a host for a dating reality show, Mr. Peronality. These ventures were short lived and received a great deal of critism.

In 1999, Monica appeared as a guest host on SNL. The skits consisted of spoofs and sly references toward her experiences with Bill Clinton.

The Washington Post also covered her SNL performance.

The same year, Monica attempted to break into the fashion industry by designed a handbag line. The Daily Beast recently reviewed the line with a modern day criticism.

In 2003, Monica became the host of reality television dating show, Mr. Personality.
