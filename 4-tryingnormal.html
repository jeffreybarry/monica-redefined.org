Monica Redefined

Trying to Live a Normal Life

What did she do after the scandal?

Struggling to regain her footing after the publicity of the trial and affair, Monica experimented with several different business ventures in the late 1990s and early 2000s. She served as a guest host on Saturday Night Live. She launched her own handbag line. And she hosted a dating reality show, Mr. Personality. The ventures didn't last and were widely panned by critics.

In 1999, Monica appeared as a guest on SNL. The skits consisted of spoofs and sly references to her relationship with Bill Clinton.

The Washington Post also covered her SNL performance.

The same year, Monica attempted to break into the fashion industry by designing a handbag line. The Daily Beast recently revisited the launch of the handbag line.
