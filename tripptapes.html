The Tripp Tapes

Linda Tripp, a key witness who befriended and betrayed Lewinsky by recording nearly 20 hours of personal conversation over the course of three months, was integral in exposing the scandal. Working in the Pentagon’s public affairs office, the then 49-year old served as a mentor to Lewinsky before turning over the tapes to Ken Starr.
